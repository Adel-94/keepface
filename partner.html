Whom we want to see as partners?

As an Keepface Partner, you’ll help identify your own clients that could benefit from Keepface, and in turn, earn commission through our revenue sharing program. Simply submit an application to tell us a bit about your client(s), and we will be in touch!
Our goal is simple: to help you make influencer marketing easy & scalable for your clients... and get rewarded for it.
Remember, we’re just here to make you - and your brand - look good.

What’s in it for you?

Revenue sharing, flexibility, and - of course - street cred.
You’ve built strong relationships with your clients, and now you can enjoy the fruits of your labour, and help them scale their campaigns, without all of the heavy lifting.

Who are we looking for?

Our Partnership Program is built for companies with clients or colleagues focused on expanding their influencer campaign reach, around the globe. Knowledge is power, so if you believe Keepface would be a good fit for your clients, we want to reward you for it.

How do I get started?

1) Sign up for the Keepface Partnership Program in the form to your right, and tell us a little bit about yourself.
2) You’ll receive a confirmation email asking you to schedule a time to talk to our team.
3) Once your application has been reviewed and accepted, we’ll send your referral package, so you can start sharing Keepface with your clients!
